Meet the Developer

Percival Salvador

Solo Developer

I'm a passionate game developer skilled in Unity and C#, with a particular love for crafting 2D games. My artistic side comes through in my pixel art and digital paintings, which I use to give my games a distinct visual flair. Beyond coding and art, I also explore composing music for my projects, adding a personalized soundscape to my creations. My goal is to create immersive gaming experiences that blend engaging gameplay with unique visuals and sound. With each project, I aim to push my skills further and bring something fresh to the gaming world.
